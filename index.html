/ 03Why it exists

Proof that "Made in New York" isn't a slogan — it's a roster.

Obviously NYC's job is to make the case that New York is a real tech city, in a category of one. The directory is the piece that ties it together: a living, browsable artifact you can send to anyone — a founder, a reporter, a potential partner — that says here are the people building here. Look.

It's deliberately curated, not open. Anyone can nominate, but every entry is hand-picked. That keeps the signal high and makes it something people actually want to be on.

And it compounds. AI is reshaping who gets to build and how fast, and right now no one is telling that story as ours. The directory plants that flag — cheaply, credibly, and in a format that grows on its own.
